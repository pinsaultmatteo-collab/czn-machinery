# New or used mini excavator: which to choose?

A full comparison: real costs, the hidden costs of used machines, hour-meter fraud, an inspection checklist and total cost of ownership over 5 years.

⏱ 11 min read
✓ CZN Machinery advice
↻ Updated 2026

The used mini excavator market is active in France (fleet renewals, liquidations, imports from northern Europe). Prices range from €5,000 to €35,000 depending on weight, hours and condition. It looks like a bargain — the reality is more nuanced once the real cost of ownership is analysed.

## The used market

Three types of used machines: branded European used (Kubota, Yanmar, Bobcat — the most reliable but the most expensive, €15,000 to €40,000), first-generation Chinese used (€6,000 to €15,000, with no warranty or parts in France), and used machines refurbished by a serious importer (inspected, 3-to-6-month warranty — the best used option).

## The hidden costs of used machines

Wear parts : at 2,000–4,000 h, tracks (€800 to €2,500), hoses (€200 to €800), bucket (€100 to €400). Ageing hydraulics : a leaking cylinder = €300–600 per cylinder; a pump to recondition = €1,000 to €3,000. Downtime on breakdown : with no warranty, €300 to €600 of lost income per day stopped. Transport : €500 to €1,500 (included at CZN).

## Hour-meter fraud

Tampering with the hour meter is common: all else equal, a machine at 1,500 h is worth 30 to 50% more than one at 4,000 h. There is no official hours register in France. To spot tampering: demand documented maintenance invoices (12 to 15 oil-change invoices over 3 years), look at the actual wear (seat, joysticks, paint), inspect the tracks , and have the hydraulic oil analysed (€200 to €400) to estimate wear independently of the meter.

## Pre-purchase inspection checklist

### Visual inspection

- Tracks: lug wear, rollers, sprocket

- Cylinders: scratches on the rods, seal leaks

- Hoses: cracking, weeping

- Structure: cracks, repair welds (a sign of an accident)

- Bucket: tooth wear, base edge

- Hydraulic oil: black oil = lack of maintenance

### At start-up and in operation

- Cold start with no excessive white smoke

- Smoke: black = overload/injectors; persistent white = head gasket

- No knocking, whistling or rattling

- Smooth hydraulic movements, even 360° rotation

- Travel: tracks in sync, no drift

### Documents to request

- A dated, signed service log and service invoices

- CE declaration of conformity (mandatory)

- Original manual, ownership history
