# Privacy policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal data, in accordance with the GDPR.

Last updated: 30 May 2026

## Data collection

CZN Machinery collects personal data only in connection with quote requests, orders and contact forms. The information collected may include: last name, first name, email address, phone number and postal address.

## Use of data

Your data is used exclusively to:

- process your quote requests and orders;

- provide sales follow-up and after-sales service;

- inform you about our products and offers, with your consent.

## Data retention

Your data is kept for a maximum of 3 years after the last contact, in accordance with applicable regulations.

## Cookies

This site uses:

- Essential cookies — required for the site to work (session, cart). Always active, no consent required.

- Analytics cookies — audience measurement (Google Analytics), anonymised data. Subject to your consent.

- Marketing cookies — advertising conversion tracking (Meta Pixel). Subject to your consent.

Cookies are kept for a maximum of 13 months. You can accept or refuse non-essential cookies at any time.

## Your rights

In accordance with the GDPR, you have the following rights: access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection and restriction of processing. To exercise them, contact us at [contact@czn-machinery.com](mailto:contact@czn-machinery.com).

## Contact

For any question regarding the protection of your personal data: [contact@czn-machinery.com](mailto:contact@czn-machinery.com).
